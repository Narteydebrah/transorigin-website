Our Foundation

Built on Institutional Experience, Driven by Continental Impact

TransOrigin Advisory Ltd provides strategic trade governance and customs modernization advisory services across West Africa and the continent. We bridge policy, practice, and technology to help governments, institutions, and private sector actors navigate complex trade systems.

Our work draws from deep institutional knowledge of customs operations, tariff administration, rules of origin, and regional trade frameworks‚Äîfrom ECOWAS protocols to the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA).

We partner with multilateral organizations, development agencies, and private sector leaders to design, implement, and optimize trade facilitation systems that promote compliance, efficiency, and regional integration.

Jonathan Dabrah

Founder & Principal Advisor

Jonathan Dabrah brings 37 years of senior leadership in customs and trade policy to TransOrigin Advisory. As former Head of Tariff & Trade at Ghana Customs, he led critical reforms in tariff classification, rules of origin implementation, and trade systems modernization.

His institutional experience spans policy development with the World Customs Organization (WCO), capacity building programs with GIZ and PwC, and advisory roles in AfCFTA and ECOWAS trade initiatives. Jonathan combines technical depth with strategic vision to deliver pragmatic, high-impact solutions.
